Los Altos Hills Youth Commission

Enfranchising the Youth of Los Altos Hills

The Los Altos Hills Youth Commission (LAHYC) is a group of Los Altos Hills teens who seek to enfranchise the youth of the area by providing them with an opportunity to connect with one another. We regularly host events to foster interaction and build connections. In addition, the LAHYC regularly volunteers at Los Altos Hills events. Over the course of the year, the Los Altos Hills Youth Commission holds 3 main events (The Monster Bash, The Leadership Conference, and The Movie Night) to help local teens meet activist, entrepreneurs, and other teens in the area.
